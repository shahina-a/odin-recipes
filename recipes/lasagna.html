Lasagna

The World's Best Lasagna Recipe!

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with 'The Best Lasagna Recipe'. Layers of rich, savory goodness await you in this classic Italian dish. With a blend of cheese, meat, and homemade sauce, it's a taste of home you won't want to miss.

Ingridients

  • Lean ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. MAKE THE MEAT SAUCE
  2. COOK THE NOODLES
  3. MAKE THE RICOTTA MIXTURE
  4. LAYER THE LASAGNA IN THE FOLLOWING ORDER:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the reamining Parmesan.
  5. COVER WITH FOIL AND BAKE

    The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes.

  6. LET THE LASAGNA REST BEFORE SERVING
