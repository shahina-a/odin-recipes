Mushroom Chicken Fettuccine

Source: TASTY

Best Creamy Mushroom Chicken Fettuccine Recipe!

Tender chicken breast, sauteed mushrooms, and fetuccine pasta are luxuriously coated in a rich velvety cream sauce. It's a gourmet delight that'll transport your taste buds to culinary bliss.

Ingridients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced thinly into strips
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons pepper
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup parsley, chopped
  • 12 ounces cooked fettucine/li>

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot over high heat.
  2. Add the chicken and let it brown in the pan, cooking until no pink is showing.
  3. Add the mushrooms, onions, garlic, salt, and pepper, cooking until mushrooms are slightly browned and onions are translucent and soft.
  4. Add the heavy cream and parsley, stirring until evenly mixed.
  5. Add the fettuccine and stir until the pasta is evenly coated.
